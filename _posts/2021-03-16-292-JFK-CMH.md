Welcome aboard of United Airlines DL4363 flight. In New York John Fitzgerald Kennedy International Airport, the sky is overcast, the temperature is 12<sup>o</sup>C. In John Glenn Columbus International Airport, the sky is clear with few clouds. The temperature is around 4<sup>o</sup>C.

During this flight, the wings and nose of the aircraft were left with ice, despite the anti-ice being turned on.
