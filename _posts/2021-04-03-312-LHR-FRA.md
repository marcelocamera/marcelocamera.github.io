Welcome onboard of flight LH909 operated by Lufthansa. This flight is from London (LHR) to Frankfurt (FRA) using the Airbus A320NEO. It is expected a flight time around 1 hour and 40 minutes.
<!--more-->

In London at Heathrow Airport, the sky is overcast, the temperature is around 9<sup>o</sup>C. In Frankfurt Airport, the sky is clear with no cloud, the temperature is around 12<sup>o</sup>C.
