Welcome onboard of flight IB8764 operated by Iberia - Lineas Aéreas de España. This flight is departuring from Madrid (MAD) to Lisbon (LIS) using the Airbus A320NEO. It is expected a flight time around 1 hour.
<!--more-->

In Madrid, at Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport, the sky is clear with no clouds, the temperature is around 21<sup>o</sup>C. In Lisbon, at Humberto Delgado Airport, the sky is scattered, the temperature is around 20<sup>o</sup>C.
