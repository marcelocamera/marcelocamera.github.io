Welcome onboard of flight LH232 operated by Lufthansa. This flight is from Frankfurt (FRA) to Rome (FCO) using the Airbus A320NEO. It is expected a flight time around 1 hour and 40 minutes.
<!--more-->

In Frankfurt at Frankfurt Airport, the sky is scattered, the temperature is around 2<sup>o</sup>C. In Rome, at Fiumicino Airport, the sky is scattered, the temperature is around 13<sup>o</sup>C.
