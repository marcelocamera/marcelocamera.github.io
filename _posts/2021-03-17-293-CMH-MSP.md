Welcome aboard of Delta Airlines DL5011 flight from Columbus (CMH) to Minneapolis (MSP). The flight time will be 1 hour and 40 minutes. The flight will be operated by Airbus A320neo.
<!--more-->

The weather in John Glenn Columbus International Airport is good, the sky is clear with few clouds, the temperature is 18<sup>o</sup>C. In Minneapolis–Saint Paul International Airport, the sky is overcast with mist. The temperature is 2<sup>o</sup>C.

In the final of the approach, I suffered a strong side wind, causing a strong touchdown.
