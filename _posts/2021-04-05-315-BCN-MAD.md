Welcome onboard of flight IB1035 operated by Iberia - Lineas Aéreas de España. This flight is departuring from Barcelona (BCN) to Madrid (MAD) using the Airbus A320NEO. It is expected a flight time around 1 hour.
<!--more-->

In Barcelona, at El Prate Airport, the sky is clear with no clouds, the temperature is around 12<sup>o</sup>C. In Madrid, at Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport, the sky is clear, the temperature is around 11<sup>o</sup>C.
